  1. From the three methods (best subset, forward stepwise, and backward stepwise):

    a. Which of the three models with k predictors has the smallest training RSS?

    Best subset selection has the smallest training RSS as it fits every combination of predictors while backside and forward stepwise models depend on which predictors they pick first.

    b. Which of the three models with k predictors has the smallest test RSS?

    Best subset may have the smallest test RSS because it considers more models. However it also may be overfitting the training data, and one of the other models may find a better model for the test data.
